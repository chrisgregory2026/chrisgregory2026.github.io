Katherine Johnson

Katherine Johnson (American Mathematician) was born in Aug. 26, 1918 and died recently in Feb. 24, 2020. Here are some pretty notable facts about her!

  • Was the first woman in the FRD to receive credit in a research report as an author.
  • Has done the orbital equations for a computer BY HAND!
  • Was with NASA (NACA back then) since June 1953 and retired 1986.
  • Integrated as one of three black students at West Virginia’s graduate schools (in the early 20th century)! An achievement for sure.
